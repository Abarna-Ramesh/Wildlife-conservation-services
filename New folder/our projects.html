Our Projects

We are dedicated to preserving and protecting wildlife through innovative conservation projects.

Our projects mainly focuses on :

1) Safeguarding Endangered Species
2) Protecting Wildlife Habitats
3) Combatting Poaching and Illegal Wildlife Trade
4) Education and Awareness Campaigns

Rhino Conservation

This project focuses on saving the rhino population, which is facing extinction due to poaching and habitat loss. We work with local communities and government agencies to increase the rhino population and secure their habitats.

Elephant Protection

This project aims to protect the elephant population from poaching, habitat loss, and ivory trade. We work with local communities and government agencies to protect elephant habitats and prevent poaching through education and awareness campaigns.

Marine Wildlife Conservation

This project focuses on preserving marine wildlife, including sea turtles, whales, and dolphins. We work with local communities and government agencies to protect marine habitats and prevent wildlife from being hunted or trapped in fishing gear.
